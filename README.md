## ❓ What’s an Agent?

**Formal definition**

**An agent is an entity that perceives its environment, decides what to do, and acts toward a goal.**  

**严格定义**  
**Agent 是一个能够感知环境、做出决策，并朝着目标采取行动的实体。**

---

**Plain explanation**

**In simple words:**  
An agent looks at the world, makes a decision, and moves step by step toward a goal.

**通俗理解：**  
简单来说，Agent 会观察世界、做决定，然后一步一步朝目标前进。
